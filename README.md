# Data-Analysis-for-Uber-Project
Uber is an American multinational transportation company that provides ride-hailing services, courier services, food delivery, and freight transport. It is headquartered in San Francisco, California, and operates in approximately 70 countries and 10,500 cities worldwide.
# Project Overview
This project analyzes Uber ride data to understand patterns in ride bookings, including categories, purposes, time trends, and distances traveled. The goal is to extract actionable insights that can help Uber optimize services, improve marketing strategies, and enhance customer experience.

# Objectives of the Project
1.	In which category do people book the most Uber rides?
2.	For which purpose do people book Uber rides the most?
3.	At what time do people book cabs the most from Uber?
4.	In which months do people book Uber rides less frequently?
5.	On which day of the week do people book Uber rides the most?
6.	How many miles do people usually book a cab for through Uber?

# Key Insights
✔ Business users dominate Uber rides → Focus on corporate partnerships.
✔ Meetings & meals are top purposes → Offer bundled ride packages for professionals.
✔ Afternoons & Wednesdays are peak times → Increase driver supply.
✔ Most rides are short-distance → Optimize pricing for 5-10 mile trips.
